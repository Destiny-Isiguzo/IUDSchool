frequently asked questions

Find all the essential information you need in our FAQ section, designed to address the most frequently asked questions and help you make informed decisions for your child's education.

What are the school's hours of operation?

Our school hours are from 8:00 AM to 3:00 PM, Monday to Friday. We also offer extended care options for parents who need early drop-off or late pick-up.

What extracurricular activities are available for students?

We offer a wide range of extracurricular activities for students, including sports, music, art, and clubs. Our goal is to provide students with opportunities to explore their interests and develop new skills.

How do I apply for admission to IUDSchool?

To apply for admission to IUDSchool, you will need to complete an online application form and submit the required documents. You can find more information about the application process on our website.
